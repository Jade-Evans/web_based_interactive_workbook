RESOURCE LIBRARY

This page can essentially act as a bookmark for certain topics. I'm thinking potentially a section in the modules pages for noting down the reference and the topic used, that then is auto generated into this page, with a search bar. So for example, a user finds a good explanation of how to ensure tables don't have padding, and wants to save to reference later, they can enter this in somewhere on the modules page with a link and submit it to resources, where it is searchable by keyword. its another way for learners to customise resources that worked specifically for them.
